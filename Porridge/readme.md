# Porridge

A combination of a variety of porridge/oatmeal recipes.

## Jen's oatmeal

### INGREDIENTS

  - 1/2 cup quinoa
  - 1/2 cup steel cut oats
  - 1/2 cup normal oats
  - 1.5 cups milk
  - 3 cups water

### INSTRUCTIONS

 1. Do this in the instant pot or rice cooker.
 1. Rinse the quinoa in a sieve and then toast in coconut oil over medium heat for a few minutes.
 1. Remove the quinoa (put into cooking pot), add more coconut oil and lightly toast the steel cut oats until golden and fragrant.
 1. Add all of the oats into the cooking pot.
 1. Add a bit of salt, then add all of the liquids.
 1. For the instant pot, pressure cook on 'oats' or in the rice cooker choose the porridge/oatmeal setting.

 ### RECOMMENDED TOPPINGS

- Honey
- Fruit (strawberries)

## Travelodge (creamy) porridge

### INGREDIENTS

  - 180g milk (2 or 3%)
  - 140g water
  - 50g rolled oats

### INSTRUCTIONS

 1. Simmer oats in water for a few minutes.
 1. Add the milk, simmer until oats cooked through.
 1. Keep stirring as it cooks.
 1. Serve once oats are cooked and creamy.

### RECOMMENDED TOPPINGS

- Lyle's maple golden syrup
- Fruit
