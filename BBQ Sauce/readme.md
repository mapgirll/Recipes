# INGREDIENTS

You may want to double or triple these amounts for more sauce.

**BBQ Sauce**
  - 2 garlic cloves, minced
  - 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  - 1.5 cup ketchup
  - 1/2 cup brown sugar
  - 2 tsp black pepper
  - 2 tsp onion powder
  - 2 tsp mustard powder
  - 1 tsp cayenne (Jon prefers 1/4 tsp of cayenne)
  - 1 tbsp worcester sauce

# INSTRUCTIONS

 1. Mix all sauce ingredients together in a dutch oven or saucepan.
 1. Once they're mixed well you can start to simmer the sauce. 
 1. Cook for a while until the sauce thickens.
 1. Take it off the heat. The sauce will continue to thicken as it cools.
