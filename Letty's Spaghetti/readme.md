# Letty's Spaghetti

## Rating:
*unrated*

# INGREDIENTS

  - 1lb spaghetti
  - 1 tbsp cooking oil
  - 4 pcs hotdogs (prefer filipino hot dogs as they're sweeter)
  - 1 onion chopped
  - 3 gloves garlic minced
  - 1 'normal' pack of ground beef (500g ish?)
  - 2 cups tomato sauce
  - 1 small can tomato paste
  - 1 cup of ketchup (prefer banana ketchup)
  - 1 beef stock cube 
  - 2 tsp sugar (assume normal white sugar)

# INSTRUCTIONS

 1. Cook pasta per instructions...
 1. Slice up hot dogs and saute them in a pan. Set aside.
 1. Cook onions and garlic in oil. Once softened, add beef. Stir until cooked and add pepper, salt and beef bouillon cube.
 1. Add tomato paste, ketchup, sauce. If it's too thick, add a little water.
 1. Add the sliced hotdogs from before.
 1. Add sugar.
 1. Stir/mix as you go. Salt and pepper to taste.
 1. Serve over spaghetti with cheese on top (if you want).
